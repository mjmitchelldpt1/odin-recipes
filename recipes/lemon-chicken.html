Lemon Pepper Chicken Quarters by MALCOLM REED

Steps

  1. Grill Chicken at 425 until internal temp 180 Degrees
  2. Plate and spoon lemon herb sauce over chicken
  3. Sprinkle chopped parsley over chicken
  4. Attempt to not eat every piece of chicken you cooked in one sitting
  5. Realize step 4 is impossible
  6. Nap after food coma
