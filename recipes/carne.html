The Best Carne Asada Recipe

Carne by Kenji

  • Carne Asada Sauce
  • Flank Steak
  • Corn Tortilla
  • Cotija Cheese

Steps

  1. Marinate steak for 2-12 hours in carne asada sauce
  2. Grill flank steak to desired doneness
  3. Let steak rest and slice
  4. Assemble corn tortilla with meat and toppings drizzle left over sauce on top
